# Aurora Hotel Policies

## Cancellation

Reservations may be cancelled without charge until 6:00 PM local hotel time two days before arrival. Later cancellations are charged one night plus applicable tax. Prepaid promotional rates are non-refundable.

## Check-In And Check-Out

Check-in begins at 3:00 PM and standard check-out is 11:00 AM. Early arrival and late departure depend on same-day availability and may carry an additional charge.

## Parking

Self-parking costs $28 per night and includes unlimited exits. Valet parking costs $42 per night. Electric vehicle charging is available on a first-come basis.

## Pets

Aurora Hotel welcomes up to two dogs per room. Each dog must weigh no more than 50 pounds. A non-refundable cleaning fee of $75 applies per stay. Service animals are exempt from the fee.

## Accessibility

Accessible rooms can include roll-in showers, visual alarms, lowered fixtures, and accessible routes. Guests should request required features before booking so availability can be confirmed.

## Breakfast

Breakfast is served from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM. It is included only when the selected rate explicitly lists breakfast. Otherwise, the buffet costs $24 per adult and $12 per child under 12.
